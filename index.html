About Us

Welcome to Brigado Burgers, Tampa's premier destination for authentic halal smash burgers. We're passionate about serving up the juiciest, most flavorful burgers made with 100% halal beef and fresh ingredients.

Every burger is smashed to perfection on our hot griddle, creating that signature crispy crust while keeping the inside tender and juicy. Come experience the Brigado difference!
